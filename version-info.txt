<p>The Matrix tab/views plug-ins provide several spreadsheet-style views of an ontology,
including existential fillers, individual relationships, and an object properties view.
You can use drag and drop to add columns and values.

<p><a href="http://code.google.com/p/co-ode-owl-plugins/wiki/MatrixViews">Documentation</a>
